One field in particular that I like is computer graphics. A game I would like to thank for that is Minecraft. When I was younger, I was hesitant to play it because of its "poor graphics" especially since I was playing games like Labyrinth 3D, Angry Birds, and Cut The Rope on my tablet which looked so much better to my eye. I ended up playing Minecraft anyway only to find myself enjoying exploring the blocky surroundings and mining diamonds despite my previous opinions. Perhaps, I still wasn't completely fond of the visual aspects of Minecraft because I found myself about different mods one of them being Optifine. Optifine enhances the graphics by making the Minecraft world look more realistic and that mostly has to do with how light is perceived by our eyes. The enhancement is not on the same level as raytracing, but it made me Raytracing is fantastical, while the idea for it may have originated centuries ago today we are finally able to see it in work in realtime as our hardware has caught up to our ambitions. Saying Minecraft looks like a different game with raytracing would be an understatement, it's a whole other world! When I was younger I used mods like Optifine to enhance the graphicswhich would Raytracing in Minecraft makes me feel like I'm playing a completely different game. The dynamic lighting that Unreal Engine 5 has is crazy impressive.
